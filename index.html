Hi! I am Kaelynn! I am currently a 22 year old student. I am from Nine Mile Falls, Wa, but I currently live in Cheney, Wa with my fiancé and turtle. We hope to get a dog soon as well.

I am a senior at Eastern Washington University. I am currently double majoring in music performance and visual communication design. This is a great work load but is a very fun challenge. I have almost completed my music degree and have about a year left on my design degree.

I am not completely sure as to what I want to do for the rest of my life, but I have many ideas and possibilities I am interested in. I am mainly interested in web design as well as video game designing.

II have a lot of hobbies, two of my favorite things just so happen to be my majors. I also love playing video games, drawing, hanging out with my friends and especially love watching videos on Youtube. I am always open to trying new things and aim to live an eventful and fun life.
